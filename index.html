I am human.
What does this mean?

I am an animal. Yet, unlike animals, every day I seek to transcend my basic nature. This quest to change the inner world I will call Spirituality.

I live immersed in an invisible world of collective symbols and fictions that guide my every thought and move. This virtual world of shared symbols I will call Culture.

I augment my influence over the physical world through systems of knowledge and tools. This extension of power over the material world I will call Technology.

These are not the only things that characterise a Human, but they might be the most relevant to the time I inhabit: the 21st Century, an age both exhilarating and terrifying.

I will, therefore, explore all three together. Every project you see here will be an effort to increase technological skills, cultural awareness, and spiritual growth at once. Through each project, I alter myself and write my future: this much is in my hands right now.
